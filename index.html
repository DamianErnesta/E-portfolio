My dream is to be an electrical engineer capable of dealing with the new concepts, serving honestly, and using sustainable practices to solve the difficult engineering problems. It is my desire to actually contribute in the society through constant learning and personal development as an individual and as a professional.

As a final-year Electrical and Electronics Engineering student at Asia Pacific University, I have gained extensive experience through real-world technical projects and innovative engineering solutions.
